High level vision (what are we selling)

Smart Care is a premium module .... Smart Care is a self-learning platform that enables brands to foster customer service by automatically responding to customer queries based on text analysis using natural language processing, thus reducing cost of delivering timely response to customer inquiries.

This platform is integrated with the knowledge base to find relevant information and compose responses.. It will continuously evolve by learning from all communications between customer support teams and customers and further refine its analysis.

Possible use cases for Smart Care: Customer Service: Automate tasks across multiple channels and reduce costs while providing a sense of personalization for consumers. Marketing & Sales: Identify potential sales and marketing leads from incoming communication channels. Live Chat: Automated chat agent across products and websites. Community: Automated reply to certain customer questions on community forums. Live Call Analysis: Analyze customer support phone conversations to provide live inputs about customer to agents.

Target use cases for Sprinklr Smart Care: Customer Service

Product Benefits: Reduced costs of customer service due to automation of email response. Reduced time taken by agents to reply to customers with automatic suggestions and information provided to agents based on text analysis.
